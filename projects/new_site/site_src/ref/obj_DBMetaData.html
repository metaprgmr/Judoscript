 Returns a TableData for the row identifiers matching tablePattern, scope and nullable. The tablePattern is a pattern in the form of catalog.schema.pattern. The scope can be one of these: "bestRowNotPseudo" (default), "bestRowPseudo", "bestRowSession", "bestRowTemporary" and "bestRowTransaction"; nullable is a boolean, default is true .
